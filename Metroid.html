Metroid NES Reimplementation

Complete reimplementation of the first region of the 1986 NES game + a custom level built around a novel custom mechanic.

Task:

Collaborate in a team of 2 to submit a milestone in 7 days, an alpha in 7 days and a gold in 5 days.

Our Process:

My partner Michelle and I started the development process by playing the original game. We then created a list of tasks, assigned priority to them, estimated effort and divided them between ourselves. Some programming we completed remotely while remaining in communication, but most development we compeleted together in person. After each sprint we met to discuss what we needed to work on and focus on next, revaluated how we defined our tasks, priorities and effort estimations, and then repeated the process. We followed essentially the same process for each subsequent project in the course, be they individual or group projects.

My Contributions:

Custom level room design, custom level novel mechanic design, custom level original enemies design

Reflections:

Invest in organising your codebase from the get go, communicate consistently with your partner be it simply communicating progress, asking when you need technical help, discussing a new idea or concern or brainstorming an approach to a problem, train yourself to pay attention to small details, in the aesthetics, mechanics, etc, they add up and contribute tremendous value to your final result.
