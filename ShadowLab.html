Our Process:

My Contributions:

  • Player decisions, and level progression design and implementation
  • Narrative and script
  • Enemy design, mechanics and implementation
  • "Set design and props" for game world

Reflections:

  • Creativity is hard
  • Your teammates are the best resources for technical growth, for helping keep you on track and accountable, for refining and growing your ideas, and for the occasional chocolate chai.
  • Take ownership of what you build and build something you would love to use.
  • Total agreement on everything is not normal. Talk it out.
  • (Reiterating) Invest in organising your codebase from the get go.
  • (Reiterating) Communicate consistently with your partner be it simply communicating progress, asking when you need technical help, discussing a new idea or concern, or brainstorming an approach to a problem.
